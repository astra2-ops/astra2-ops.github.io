A.S.T.R.A. Collective

Welcome to ASTRA (ATL Sans Tinkerer Alliance), an independent collective under Hack Club USA (A International Organisation) dedicated to pushing the boundaries of robotics and innovation.

Join Us

Our Mission

ASTRA's mission is to create a vibrant and collaborative community that supports and advances the projects and objectives of the ATL Lab. We believe that everyone can be a part of the innovation journey, regardless of their background or experience.

Mission Statement

  • Foster a collaborative environment for innovators and tinkerers.
  • Support and enhance the ATL Lab’s projects and goals.
  • Provide resources, guidance, and opportunities for members to grow and contribute to cutting-edge robotics and technology.

Key Objectives

  • Innovation and Collaboration: Encourage creative thinking and teamwork among members to drive forward groundbreaking projects.
  • Skill Development: Offer resources, workshops, and mentorship to help members develop their skills and expertise.
  • Community Building: Create a supportive network where members can share ideas, seek advice, and collaborate on projects.

How ASTRA Operates

Membership

Eligibility: Open to individuals interested in robotics, technology, and innovation. Members should be enthusiastic about contributing to and learning from the collective.

Application: Interested candidates can apply through our membership form. //// Anyone can join the open community!

Activities and Programs

  • Workshops and Seminars: Regular events covering various aspects of robotics and technology.
  • Project Collaborations: Opportunities for members to work on joint projects, including those directly linked to ATL Lab objectives.
  • Mentorship Programs: Guidance from experienced members and professionals in the field.
  • Get Grants for Projects: Get money grants and help on your project from Hack Club and GitHub EDU.

Communication and Support

  • Online Platform: Access to our online forum and resources for discussions, updates, and support.
  • Regular Meetings: Scheduled meetings to discuss ongoing projects, new ideas, and club activities.
  • Support Channels: Direct support from club leaders and mentors for project guidance and personal development.

How to Get Involved

  • Join Us: Be a part of the community. Anyone can join! Join Us
  • Participate: Attend our workshops, collaborate on projects, and contribute to discussions.
  • Stay Updated: Follow our announcements and updates to stay informed about upcoming events and opportunities.
